# Business Introduction

Healthcare organizations generate large volumes of operational and financial data through patient admissions, treatment records, insurance processing, and billing systems. However, without structured analytics, this data often remains underutilized, limiting the organization’s ability to identify revenue opportunities, understand patient demographics, and improve operational performance.

Trich Healthcare operates across multiple medical departments and processes thousands of patient transactions annually. Leadership requires clear insights into:

- Revenue performance
- Patient demographics
- Departmental financial contribution
- Payment behaviour patterns
- Insurance utilization trends

To support better decision-making, this project develops a **Power BI healthcare analytics dashboard** that consolidates patient, revenue, and operational data into a centralized visual reporting system.

The objective is to transform raw healthcare data into **actionable business intelligence that supports operational efficiency and revenue optimization.**

---

# Problem Statement

Trich Healthcare management currently faces limited visibility into key operational and financial metrics due to fragmented data sources and manual reporting processes.

Several operational questions remain difficult to answer:

- Which hospital departments generate the highest revenue?
- How does revenue fluctuate across the year?
- Which payment methods are most commonly used by patients?
- What role do insurance types play in revenue distribution?
- Are there demographic patterns in healthcare utilization?
- How can leadership monitor patient transactions efficiently?

Without a centralized analytics system, leadership struggles to:

- Monitor financial performance in real time
- Identify operational inefficiencies
- Detect demand patterns across departments
- Make data-driven strategic decisions

This project addresses these challenges by designing a **Power BI healthcare intelligence dashboard** that consolidates operational healthcare data into a single analytical environment.

---

# Project Overview

This project focuses on building an **interactive healthcare analytics dashboard using Microsoft Power BI** to monitor patient activity, revenue performance, and healthcare service utilization.


The dashboard enables stakeholders to:

- Track total revenue generated from healthcare services
- Monitor total patient volume and transaction counts
- Understand departmental revenue contribution
- Analyze payment behaviour and insurance patterns
- Identify demographic trends in healthcare usage

The solution provides **executive-level KPIs, interactive visualizations, and detailed patient records**, allowing both strategic and operational decision-making.

---

# Dataset Overview

The dataset used in this project contains patient healthcare transaction records with the following attributes:

| Column | Description |
|------|-------------|
| UserID | Unique patient identifier |
| TransactionID | Unique medical transaction ID |
| Gender | Patient gender |
| City | Patient location |
| Age | Patient age |
| InsuranceType | Insurance coverage category |
| ChronicCondition | Recorded chronic health condition |
| Department | Hospital department providing treatment |
| TransactionDate | Date of service |
| PaymentMethod | Payment method used |

This dataset supports both **financial analytics and patient demographic insights.**

# Revenue Trend Analysis

The revenue trend chart illustrates how healthcare revenue fluctuates throughout the year.

### Observations

- Revenue peaks during **August and March**, indicating periods of higher healthcare demand.
- Revenue declines during **June and February**, suggesting seasonal variation in healthcare utilization.
- A cyclical pattern indicates predictable fluctuations in patient demand.

### Business Insight

Understanding seasonal demand allows healthcare administrators to:

- Allocate staff efficiently
- Manage operational resources
- Prepare departments for peak patient volumes
